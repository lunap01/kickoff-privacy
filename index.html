Privacy Policy for Kickoff

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Kickoff does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All team, player, match, and season information you enter is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to us or to any third party.

In-app purchases

Kickoff offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock the full app. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store; we do not receive or store your payment information.

Notifications

Kickoff may ask permission to send local notifications (for example, a half-time reminder). These are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device and involve no data transmission.

Analytics and tracking

Kickoff does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking software of any kind.

Children's privacy

Kickoff may be used by coaches to track youth players' names and match statistics. This information is stored locally on the coach's own device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Changes to this policy

If Kickoff's data practices change in a future update (for example, if optional cloud sync for club or team management is introduced), this policy will be updated accordingly before that update is released.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact pscicluna@gmail.com.
